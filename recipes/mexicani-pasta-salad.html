Mexicani Pasta Salad

Mexican styled-pasta salad that is great for a picnic.

This creamy Mexican Pasta Salad marks the start of the sacred season of picnics and potlucks, back porch barbecues, and days at the lake.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package tri-color rotini pasta
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-styled corn, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
  • ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
  • ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing
  • ½ cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup minced fresh cilantro
  • 1 slice onion, minced
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • ½ lime, juiced

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
  3. Drain.
  4. Mix black beans, corn, green chilies, red bell pepper, Italian dressing, Mexican cheese, green onions, cilantro, onion, taco seasoning, and lime juice together in a bowl.
  5. Add rotini and lightly stir to combine.
